# Jurisdiction and Legal Validity

How licenses work across different legal systems and regions.

## Core Concepts

| Term | Plain Explanation | Why It Matters |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Jurisdiction** | The legal authority of a particular country or region. Some licenses may not be valid in all jurisdictions. | A license may be enforceable in one country but not another |
| **Legal Recognition** | Whether a license is legally enforceable in a given jurisdiction. | Public domain dedications may not be recognized everywhere |
| **Cross-Jurisdictional** | Valid across multiple countries/legal systems. | Important for international projects |
| **Legally Valid** | Whether a license has legal force and can be enforced in court. | Determines if license terms are binding |

## Jurisdiction-Specific Licenses

| License | Jurisdiction | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **EUPL-1.2** | European Union | Designed for EU law. Legally valid across all EU member states. |
| **Unlicense** | Varies | May not be recognized in jurisdictions that don't recognize public domain dedications. |
| **CC0-1.0** | Global (with fallback) | Designed for better cross-jurisdictional use than Unlicense. Includes fallback permissive license. |
| **GPL** | Global | Widely recognized and enforceable in most jurisdictions. |
| **MIT** | Global | Simple and widely recognized. |

## Legal Validity Concerns

| Issue | Plain Explanation | Affected Licenses |
|-------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Public Domain Recognition** | Some jurisdictions don't recognize public domain dedications, meaning the creator still holds copyright. | Unlicense, CC0 |
| **Patent Grant Enforceability** | Patent laws vary by country. A patent grant in a license may not be enforceable everywhere. | Apache-2.0, GPL-3.0 |
| **Non-Commercial Definition** | What counts as "commercial" varies by jurisdiction and is not always clearly defined. | CC-BY-NC, CC-BY-NC-SA |
| **Anti-Tivoization** | Hardware restrictions may be legal in some jurisdictions but not others. | GPL-3.0 |

## LicenseWise Jurisdiction Handling

LicenseWise asks about jurisdictional concerns to:

1. Recommend licenses with clear legal recognition
2. Avoid licenses that may not be enforceable in certain regions
3. Prefer CC0 over Unlicense for better cross-jurisdictional support
4. Consider EUPL for EU-specific projects
